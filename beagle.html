Beagle Trivia

  1. Beagles were originally bred for hunting due to their strong sense of smell.
  2. They are very friendly and great with families.
  3. Snoopy from "Peanuts" is based on a Beagle.
  4. Beagles come in different color variations, usually brown, black, and white.
  5. They are one of the most popular breeds in the world.
